Introduction

Break the routine and create your own custom BanList! By using our Custom Banlist Creator tool. It allows you to add the cards you like to the banlist and ban/limit the cards you don't like! then you can use it in the game and have fun!

Features

  • Create your own customized banlist.
  • You can add the cards to your banlist and export the new banlist file.
  • You can ban individual cards by name.
  • You can ban a gorpu of cards as well depending on their type, race, archetype, etc..
  • You can have a limited deckSize in the banList which will limit the deck applied to this banlist.
  • You can add specific number of cards to (Main, Side, extra deck) which is called "ban by Location"
  • You also have the "whitelist" option which will ban all the cards except the one you add to the list. So It will be an "allowed list" instead of "ban list"
  • You've templates for the well-known banlists, which you can apply to the tool and modify them on your own.
  • After exporting/generating your banlist. You'll get a file, that can be then imported to your game so you'll have the ability to play with your own cutsomized banlist in the game as well.

Create Your List

  1. To start, click "Create New Banlist"
  2. To add a card/group of cards to the list. Click "Add Item."
  3. In the first time, you need to specify your banlist name and whether you need it to be a white list or not.
  4. Click Next, then choose your limitation type, Limitation or Requirement.
  5. Optional: You can add the field "deckSize" here as well in case you needed a limit for the deck.
  6. Click Next, Choose the ban type (single, group). Each one of these will be explained in the rest of sections.
  7. Below, you'll find a GIF that shows the steps mentioned above, refer to it for a more visual explanation.

List of cards

  1. Starting from Step "6" in the above section. You choose "Search A Specific Card.." for banning an individual card (adding it to the list).
  2. You'll get a search field and below it you'll find a list view for card names with their artwork.
  3. In the search field write the name of the card you want. For example "Blue-Eyes White Dragon".
  4. You'll get a result of the desired search, choose the "Blue-Eyes" from the dropdown view.
  5. Below the searchField you'll find a number input field, this is the amount of limitation for the card in the deck / requirement.
  6. In this case let's specify it to (0), which means the card is banned. As we already set the type to "Limitation".
  7. Click Next, then the "Add" button should be active. Click it.
  8. You'll notice the item added below and beause it's the first time you'll find the name of the banlist in the top and the "whitelist" if you were intending to create a white list.
  9. Below, you'll find a GIF that shows the steps mentioned above, refer to it for a more visual explanation.

Group of Cards

  1. Starting from Step "6" in the above section. You choose "Card Type/ Attrbitue, etc..". In this case, I wil choose "Type".
  2. Choose the type you want to ban, for Example, "Spell".
  3. Click "Next".
  4. You will notice the "Add" button is now active, click it! Check the banlist below you'll notice that the group added.
  5. Below, you'll find a GIF that shows the steps mentioned above, refer to it for a more visual explanation.
